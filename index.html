About

Welcome to Silent Depth — the official poetry site of Rence Blunt. Here, words dive into stillness, uncovering reflections on love, life, and the unseen depths of the human spirit.

Poems

Silent Nights

The night whispers,
a language only silence knows.
In its stillness,
I find myself.

Roots

I come from soil,
yet I rise like smoke—
untethered,
searching for skies.
