Hello, I’m Rence Blunt

Poetry has always been my open space — a way to pour emotions into words, and give voice to thoughts that often stay unspoken. Through rhythm, rhyme, free verse, and other forms, I try to capture the quiet depth of human experience.

This site is a collection of my poems, reflections, and highlights. It’s a journey of self-expression, vulnerability, and creativity, and I hope that in these words you find something that speaks to you.

My Mission

To weave poetry that heals, giving voice to the unspoken, and find pacing for every heart.

My Vision

To be a guiding light in the world of poetry.

About This Platform

Volant Poetry is more than just a personal space— it’s a platform for sharing emotions, inspiring creativity, and conveying messages through the beauty of words. From poems to quotes, reflections, and thoughts, it’s designed to give readers a place of comfort, inspiration, and reflection.

Dedication

This platform is lovingly dedicated to all dreamers, poets, and seekers of meaning. To those who find comfort in words, and to every soul who has ever felt unseen — may these poems remind you that you are never alone.

Rence Blunt
